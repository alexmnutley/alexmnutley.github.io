ABOUT ME:

I am originally from Long Island, NY, and have lived there pretty much my whole life. Right now, however, I spend most of my time in Ithaca, NY at college.

I am currently studying Operations Research and Engineering at Cornell University, on track to graduate in 2022. Outside of class, I often spend my free time playing my guitar, learning about how to work on cars, or working on some graphic design. My professional interests include the fields of Quantitative Finance, Data Science, Statistics, and Programming.

No matter what I am doing, my object is always to grow as a person and learn from my experiences. I love to work in teams and receive ideas from others, and truly believe that this is the best way to do good work.

My personal and passion projects include, for one, this website! I also maintain an Instagram account solely for my music, which you can visit by clicking the music link above. Finally, I would like to thank you for visiting my website and reading this!
